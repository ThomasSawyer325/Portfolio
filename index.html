Barcode Scanner

This simple app scans barcodes to provide nutrition info for food items. Enter an amount in grams, ounces, or pounds, and it calculates details like Calories, Protein, and more for that portion.

Tech Stack:

Android Studio: Android App Development
Google ML Kit: Barcode Scanning
USDA FoodData Central: Nutrition Information Database
Open Food Facts: Nutrition Information Database

CoachAL

CoachAL is a fitness motivator app that connects to bluetooth sensors to gather ride data from stationary exercise bikes. It runs in the background, and as long as the rider is pedaling with their RPM thresholds, they can use the device normally.

Tech Stack:

Android Studio: Android App Development
GitHub: Version Control
Wahoo Pod Cadence Sensor: Data Collection
iConsole Bluetooth Sensor Data Collection
Heroku Postgres Data Storage
